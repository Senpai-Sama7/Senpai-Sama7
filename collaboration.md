## 📋 Collaboration Guidelines

### ✅ What Works Well
- **Clear Goals**: Define objectives and success metrics upfront
- **Regular Check-ins**: Weekly sync meetings and progress updates
- **Shared Tools**: GitHub, Slack, Figma, or similar platforms
- **Documentation**: Keep everything well-documented and accessible
- **Flexible Scheduling**: Respect different time zones and availability

### 🚫 What Doesn't Work
- Unclear requirements or constantly changing scope
- Poor communication or long response delays
- Proprietary code without proper licensing
- Unrealistic deadlines or crunch culture
- Lack of version control or backup strategies
